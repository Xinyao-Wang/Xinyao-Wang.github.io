About

I am a PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering at UC Riverside. My research focuses on learning-based control methods for human-assistive soft wearable robots driven by pneumatic artificial muscles. I aim to advance safe and adaptive robotic assistance for rehabilitation and daily movement support.
